How to Get to Mocra

  1. Get to our office.
    1. Drive
      1. Put 46 Douglas St, Milton, 4064 in your GPS/iPhone or failing that use Google maps directions.
      2. Drive to our office.
    2. Public transport
      1. Walk up Park Rd (250 m).
      2. Turn right at Douglas St (250 m).
      3. The Mocra office will be on the left.
  2. If you have successfully followed the above directions you will now see a two story building covered in vines.
  3. Head around to the left of the building (gravel carpark).
  4. There will be a green door to your right.
  5. Go though and head along the corridor.
  6. Head up the stairs, Mocra office is the first door on the right.
  7. Welcome to Mocra!
