How to Get to Mocra

  1. Put 46 Douglas st, Milton, 4064 in your GPS/iPhone or failing that use Google maps directions.
  2. Drive to our office.
  3. If you have successfully followed the above directions you will now see a 2 story building covered in vines.
  4. Head around to the left of the building (gravel carpark).
  5. There will be a green door to your right.
  6. Go though and head along the corridor, then take a right and double back up the stairs, turn right at the top of the stairs, the Mocra office is the first door on the right.
  7. Welcome to Mocra!
